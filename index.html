—ABOUT THE FIRM


THREAT-BASED.

Humint Security Services Private Limited is a security consultancy firm geared to redefine the security service industry in Zimbabwe. Through its highly qualified and experienced personnel, we provide bespoke security consultancy to individuals and corporates alike.

We provide real-time risk analysis, threat assessments, security consultancy, anti-illicit trade investigations, and counterfeit goods detection to help clients identify and prevent potential risks before they impact operations.

Our approach is preventive, proactive, and focused on enabling safe, uninterrupted business operations through timely intelligence and practical security solutions.


OUR MISSION


To be a leading professional security consultancy services provider by ensuring that our clients' interests, assets and operations are protected by scanning their operating environments through real time risk analysis and threat assessment.
